The definition of a website has literally gone out of the window these days. The modern explanation reads, " It's something that makes a business card look awesome ".

Though something like myawesomepipeshop.com does make an impression on your business card, it also serves other purposes. Your website is like your marketing team except that it doesn't expect a raise and works at 3 a.m. The only thing it asks of you is that you renew it annually and give it a respectable set of clothes to wear (read layout).

The Web Development Team here at B&W like to be called the Website Fashion Designers. After a detailed discussion of what your requirement is, we quickly get down to what we do best, design, crop and stitch code! We also offer optional Search Engine Optimization (SEO) provisions, which will boost your search engine rankings hence giving your company more visibility.

Have you wondered why the English keyboard has the alphabets all mixed up? Shouldn't they ideally be in the same order as they were taught to us in school? That is where the concept of "User Interface Design" comes by and says "hi!". UI Design is not just a mere cosmetic upgrade to an application. It is not just replacing flat, solid bars with new glossy panels and buttons with rounded corners.

It is a psychological understanding which involves placing oneself in the end user's shoes and looking at the product through his eyes. Nobody wants their spectacularly coded software/website to be left untouched by the online population in the dark dusty corners of the internet just because of poor interface considerations; neither do we.

Simplicity always wins. The most downloaded and the most popular applications have always been the ones that have managed to strike the right chord between efficiency and simplicity. With virtual markets being flooded with hundreds of thousands of such applications every day, mobile application development sure does mean serious business.

Have you hit on a brilliant idea for a smart phone app? Pay us a visit and let our hyperactive team of iPhone, Android and Blackberry developers weave their geeky magic and help you bring it to life. With a dedicated team of designers and UI specialists mobile app development at B&W is a fun filled experience while we try to inject tons of interactivity and color into the product at the same time not losing sight of its functionality
